Simple Rule-Based Chatbot Demo

How to Use

Look for the chat widget in the bottom-right corner of the screen. Click on the input field, type your message, and press Enter or click Send.

Try asking:

  • "What are your hours?"
  • "Where are you located?"
  • "What services do you offer?"
  • "What's your phone number?"
  • "Tell me a joke"
  • "Help"

The chatbot uses simple pattern matching to respond to common queries. You can easily extend it by adding more rules in the rules.js file.
